### Overview

<p className="mt-3 mb-5">
  We have added an automated version update system for our app. So you can do it
  very easily. From the version update page will see the current installed
  version and also you able to see the latest available version of your
  application. If the latest available version is greater than your current
  installed version in that case you can update your app.
</p>

> **Important Note:** Some requirements are shown below and you should remember that this process will take a couple of minutes.

- Don't close the tab.
- Don't reload the page.
- Should not turn off your PC
- Stay discontinued from any activities of your app.
- Just stay with this automated process.

<p className="mb-2">
